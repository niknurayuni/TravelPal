ATTRACTION & INTEREST

Van Gogh Museum

The museum contains the largest collection of Van Gogh's paintings and drawings in the world. In 2017, the museum had 2.3 million visitors and was the most-visited museum in the Netherlands, and the 23rd-most-visited art museum in the world.

Anne Frank House

The Anne Frank House is a writer's house and biographical museum dedicated to Jewish wartime diarist Anne Frank. The building is located on a canal called the Prinsengracht, close to the Westerkerk, in central Amsterdam in the Netherlands.

Keukenhof

Keukenhof, also known as the Garden of Europe, is one of the world's largest flower gardens, situated in the municipality of Lisse, in the Netherlands.



Amsterdam

"The Dam on the River Amstel is the capital and most populous city of the Netherlands.

Royal Palace Amsterdam

The Royal Palace of Amsterdam in Amsterdam is one of three palaces in the Netherlands which are at the disposal of the monarch by Act of Parliament.

Castle De Haar

It is the largest castle in The Netherlands.

Kinderdijk

First originated in its name in 1421 from a legend where a wave was said to have washed ashore a cradle that contained a sleeping baby.

Harleem

Capital of the province of North Holland.

ARTIS

It is the oldest zoo in the Netherlands and fifth oldest zoo in the world.
